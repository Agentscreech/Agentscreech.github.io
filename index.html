Background

My story begins in the late '80s where my step-father managed to have a 486DX computer. You know...the one that only ran DOS and had a monochrome output?

He was able to teach me the basics of how to use it. He also showed me how to find out what commands were available to me and how to find out how to use them

With that knowledge I found out that I could make texted based menus, draw rudementiary boxes with ASCII, and launch different programs with one button...at 8 years old

This was a true calling for me at an early age. Later, in high school, I was able to learn BASIC and wrote a program that took up the whopping 128kb memory of a TI-85 calulator. The program was a slot machine
